# Part 4 Relationships to Other Methodologies and Patterns

So far in this book you’ve learned how to use domain-driven design to design software solutions according to an organization’s business strategy and needs.

> 到目前为止，在本书中您已经学习了如何使用领域驱动设计来根据组织的业务策略和需求设计软件解决方案。

We saw how to apply DDD tools and practices to **make sense of**-理解,弄懂 the business domain, design the boundaries of the system’s components, and implement the business logic.

> 我们看到了如何应用 DDD 工具和实践来理解业务领域、设计系统组件的边界以及实现业务逻辑。

---

Domain-driven design covers a lot of the software development lifecycle, but it can’t cover all of software engineering.

> 领域驱动的设计涵盖了很多软件开发生命周期，但是它不能涵盖所有的软件工程。

Other methodologies and tools have their roles.

> 其他方法和工具也有它们的作用。

In Part IV, we will discuss DDD in relation to other methodologies and patterns:

> 在第四部分中，我们将讨论 DDD 与其他方法和模式的关系：

- It’s no secret that domain-driven design gained most of its traction-支持,关注 due to the popularity-流行,普及 of the microservices-based architectural style.

  > 众所周知，由于基于微服务的架构风格的流行，领域驱动设计获得了大部分的吸引力。

  In Chapter 14, we will explore the interplay between microservices and domain-driven design and how the two approaches complement-补充,补足 each other.

  > 在第14章中，我们将探讨微服务和领域驱动设计之间的相互作用，以及这两种方法如何相互补充。

- The event-driven architecture is a popular method of architecting scalable, performant, and resilient-有弹性的 distributed systems.

  > 事件驱动的体系结构是构建可伸缩、高性能和弹性分布式系统的一种流行方法。

  In Chapter 15, you will learn the principles of event-driven architecture and how to leverage DDD to design effective asynchronous communication.

  > 在第15章中，您将学习事件驱动架构的原理以及如何利用 DDD 来设计有效的异步通信。

- Chapter 16 concludes-结束,总结 the book with effective modeling in the context of data analytics.

  > 第16章以数据分析背景下的有效建模结束本书。

  You will learn about the predominant-占主导地位的,主要的 data management architectures, data warehouses and data lakes, and how their shortcomings-缺点,短处 are addressed by the data mesh-网格 architecture.

  > 您将了解主要的数据管理体系结构、数据仓库和数据湖，以及数据网格体系结构如何解决它们的缺点。

  We will also analyze and discuss how DDD and the data mesh architecture are based on the same design principles and goals.

  > 我们还将分析和讨论 DDD 和数据网格体系结构如何基于相同的设计原则和目标。
