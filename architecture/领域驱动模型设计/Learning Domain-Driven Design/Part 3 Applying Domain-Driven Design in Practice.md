# Part 3 Applying Domain-Driven Design in Practice

In Parts I and II, we discussed domain-driven design tools for making strategic and tactical design decisions.

> 在第1部分和第2部分中，我们讨论了用于制定战略和战术设计决策的领域驱动设计工具。

In this part of the book, we move from theory to practice.

> 在本书的这一部分，我们将从理论转向实践。

You will learn to apply domain-driven design in real-life projects.

> 您将学习在现实生活项目中应用领域驱动设计。

- Chapter 10 merges-融合,融入 what we discussed about strategic and tactical design into simple rules of thumb that streamline the process of making design decisions.

  > 第10章将我们所讨论的战略和战术设计整合为简单的经验法则，从而简化设计决策过程。

  You will learn to quickly identify patterns that match the business domain’s complexity and needs.

  > **您将学习如何快速识别与业务领域的复杂性和需求相匹配的模式。**

- In Chapter 11, we will look at domain-driven design from a different perspective.

  > 在第11章中，我们将从不同的角度来看领域驱动设计。

  Designing a great solution is important, but not enough.

  > 设计一个好的解决方案很重要，但还不够。

  We have to keep it in shape-形状 as the project evolves through time.

  > 随着项目的发展，我们必须保持它的形状。

  In this chapter, you will learn to apply domain-driven design tools to maintain and evolve software design decisions over time.

  > **在本章中，您将学习如何应用领域驱动的设计工具来维护和发展软件设计决策。**

- Chapter 12 introduces EventStorming: a hands-on activity that streamlines the process of discovering domain knowledge and building a ubiquitous language.

  > 第12章介绍了 EventStorming：一个实践活动，它简化了发现领域知识和构建通用语言的过程。

- Chapter 13 concludes-总结 Part III with a selection of tips and tricks for “gently” introducing and incorporating domain-driven design patterns and practices in brownfield projects—the kinds of projects we work on the most.

  > 第13章总结了第三部分，其中选择了一些技巧和技巧，用于“温和地”在棕地项目中引入和合并领域驱动的设计模式和实践——棕地项目是我们工作最多的项目。
